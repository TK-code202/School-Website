Academics

The entry-level class into our Secondary School is JSS1 (The Junior Secondary School), and in the first three years, our students are introduced to more advanced studies in English, Mathematics, and Sciences. At the end of the three years, our students sit for the Basic Education Certificate Examination (BECE).They are then placed in various departments according to their performance in the BECE and Aptitude Test. Our students spend three years in the Senior Secondary School before proceeding to take the West African Senior Secondary Certificate Examination (WASSCE) and the optional NECO Senior Secondary Certificate Examinations conducted by the West Acfrican Examinations Council (WAEC) and National Examinations Council, respectively.

Mininmum Age For Classes

JSS1: 11years

JSS2: 12years

SS1: 14years

SS2: 15years
